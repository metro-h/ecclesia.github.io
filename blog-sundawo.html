Growing Sales: Essentials of marketing a business

Israel Sundawo In Bahrain, Middle East Feb 2022
Comments 39K Views
Introduction

In this modern day, the majority of humanity can no longer live by salaries alone, but should run businesses for self-sustenance or to add a second or third income etcetera. The word of God states that He will bless the works of our hands. It is very important that as Christians we put our hands onto something so that God’s blessings can find somewhere to rest. For businesses to prosper, grow sales and eventually mature to become big businesses, there are basic sales & marketing essentials that any small business owner has to know and implement in this modern age. Marketing involves steps or strategies that lead a business to determine who its customers are or should be, how best to approach them, how to measure responses, how to find more customers, and how to increase the business’s bottom line. Keep in mind, more important than the number of sales is the amount of profitable sales. Below are just a few basic essentials that a business can implement which will help grow sales and profit margins;

  1. Research your market

    I have noticed that when we speak about marketing research, it sounds like something only bigger businesses do. The truth is if any large, small or new business neglects this step, it risks selling the wrong product to the wrong people in the wrong place, or at the wrong price. It is critically important that we do a market research so that we can gather relevant information about what our customers need and then we make informed business decisions. For example if your customers are your churchmates, you need to speak to them and find out exactly what kind of product or service they require or expect from you and then you supply accordingly. Customers will not buy simply because they know you and you should never try to sell what you want but what the customers want. Write an Overall marketing plan & establish an annual marketing budget This doesn’t have to be something complicated for a small business but it helps to establish a strategy to grow revenue and clearly outline the path the company needs to take. It will answer issues like target market, geographic segments to be serviced, growth strategy, distribution channels and the communications strategies to be implemented. A clear marketing plan will help us as a business to know where to go, what to do and at what

  2. Build a brand

    Many consumers or customers will buy a product or service strictly on the strength of the brand, no matter what the cost or how it actually performs. So it is very important that every small business creates or establishes a brand that will quickly come into the minds of the customers. After creating a brand, one needs to protect it at all costs and probably register it officially before someone runs ahead and take it. Branding is an important component of the product or service, contributing to its quality and perceived value. We should at least make sure all our products’ packaging are branded so that the customers know whose it is. A small business owner can actually utilize the already existing or established brand guidelines or strategies which are readily available via online platforms like google or to hire a professional to assist in the journey.

  3. Social Media Marketing

    If one wants to successfully market his/her business, he/she simply can’t ignore the wonders that come with social media marketing. Social media isn’t the be-all and end-all of marketing, but it actually does have a very special place in every modern marketing strategy. Any serious business owner should make sure that they are available on the major social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube and Twitter) and should be prepared to post content consistently. That doesn’t mean you have to post every day, but it’s important to keep up with your fans, followers or customers regularly because this keeps you top of their minds and encourages engagement and traffic. However as you use social media, it’s advisable to engage professionals who can manage your pages and use interactive posts, hashtags and probably enlist the help of influencers to increase sales and eventually profits.


  4. Trust & Integrity

    “A false balance is abomination to the Lord: but a just weight is his delight.” (Prov 11:1) Most importantly the word of God teaches us that God never lies. As a business owner, being dishonest would violate God’s character in you because He is a God of truth. He not only knows all things, but everything He communicates about what He knows is true. God calls us as His people to exemplify His character through honesty. You should have integrity as you provide goods and services to your customers, be truthful and never cheat your customers. As a business owner, you can ask yourselves these questions in relation to your sales and marketing activities:

    Is there anything unfair or deceptive about your products or services? In what way might you be exaggerating or overstating your offers? What would the customer say if they knew what you know?

  5. Conclusion

    One key important thing for business owners who are also Christians is trust & integrity. This on its own will lead to growth in sales as satisfied customers are able to actually bring more customers to the business leading to sales growth. These are just a few ways that you can implement depending on your budget and target audience, you should be able to identify which channels will be most effective in connecting you to your potential customers and also, you should have in mind that it’s better to spread your campaign across different platforms to spread your net.
