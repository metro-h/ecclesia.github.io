Opportunities And Challenges of Artificial Intelligence to Young People

TamiriraIshe Nyevera, Gokwe October 2023
Comments 50K Views
Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (hereinafter referred to as AI) basically allows computers or system to make decisions without explicit programming. Hence, it can imitate how people think, present good opportunities but can be equally problematic. So, it needs proper management to harness its cutting edge.

Opportunities of AI for Young People

  1. Education Empowerment

    AI-powered educational tools can provide personalized learning experiences, making it easier for young people to grasp complex concepts. AI has different tools, for example, the recently booming Chat-GPT. Though, it has been controversial to some lecturers and universities as some students use it in doing their assignments. Having algorithms that select or scan through the user’s favorite, this often helps users to receive blogs, updates, books or even videos concerning the areas of interest. Thus, the education is then enhanced due to such powerful algorithms. On a lighter note, when one likes a post on Facebook or YouTube, videos or posts relating to the one liked appears more often, which narrows down the scope of the content the user wants to have or has interest in.

  2. Career Opportunities

    AI skills are in high demand, and youths can acquire valuable skills for future job prospects in fields like data science and AI development. While there is a serious outcry of how robotics and AI are taking over the world as well as replacing people at workplaces, more jobs are also being created that will help govern and manage AI. This article will highlight some of the different jobs in this sector. There is data collection done by the collectors, leading to analysis of data, done by the data analyst, from there data scientist create the algorithms, there are data engineers etc. All these roles a decade ago they weren’t being even advertised especially here in Zimbabwe but as of late such posts have been in demand due to the high rise of AI.

  3. Social and Professional Connection:

    AI-powered communication platforms enable youth to connect with friends and peers worldwide, promoting cultural exchange and understanding. Platforms like LinkedIn for example have AI driven algorithms that suggest people who are in the same business or profession with you based on your profile. Some platforms like Up-work helps some people to sell their skills and business hence earn young people a living, AI will lead the rightful people/clients to your doorstep based on the given work that one young person can offer. Entertainment: AI-driven content recommendations and virtual reality experiences offer engaging entertainment options for young people. AI has given a wide option and indirectly can be a great deal to fight drug and substance abuse. Recently there has been more of a crisis in different parts of Zimbabwe and the world because of drugs due to different causes. For the young people there are different social media platforms with AI-generated algorithm that enhances one’s experience for a better or worse, as the user is given a choice. This entertainment can save young people from engaging in any form of drug abuse.

    Challenges of AI for Youths

    1. Privacy Concerns

      Youths may be more vulnerable to privacy breaches and data exploitation as AI collects and analyzes personal information. As mentioned earlier, AI deals with data, they say “the greater the amount of data you have the better the performance of any algorithm”. This breaches the privacy of an individual which leads to some content creation which might be harmful. This has been seen on the battle between the Huawei smartphone company based in China and the USA government as there were allegations to steal the American people’s data through their devices which somehow led to the banning of the Google services on some of Huawei smartphones as Google is based in USA and banning of some of those gadgets in USA. This data can help in creation of civil wars and society manipulation.

    2. Addiction

      : AI algorithms in social media and gaming can contribute to addiction and excessive screen time. This kills the production time for any young person to learn new skills or even start a business. It can lead to laziness which probably destroy the future of church and country at large. There has been reported that there is a total of a million of hours spent within a week on Tik-Tok in Africa only. Though the entertainment can help fight drug addiction it can be an addiction on its own it needs proper management.

    3. Job Disruptions

      As AI automates certain tasks, it may affect entry-level job opportunities for young workers. There is a serious need of continuous improvement in almost every sector of operation as AI can do the human jobs in less time and more accurate and from the world market perception this leaves the employers with no choice but to let go their long serving members as they are after productivity. AI achieves these feet by the use of robots, 3D printing etc.

    4. Ethical Issues

      Exposure to biased or harmful AI content and decisions can impact a young person's worldview and behavior. There is an almost defined trend of the new generation comically termed “Ama2K”, the content they are consuming has given them almost non-acceptable uniform dressing, vulgar language, pornography appetite and drug abuse, though all these were present before but they have highly trended on this young generation through how fast information is travelling on their gadgets and found consuming such content.

Conclusion

Balancing the benefits and drawbacks of AI usage is crucial to ensure that young people harness its potential while being aware of its limitations and potential pitfalls
