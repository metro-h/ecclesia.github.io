Disengage 2023 TO Engage 2024

Dr Kimion Tagwirei, Bulawayo Zimbabwe October 2023
Comments 51K Views

Introduction

As the year 2023 draws to a close and we anticipate the arrival of the New Year, 2024, it is important to acknowledge that every ending marks the beginning of something new. Whether we have experienced prosperity or faced challenges, found joy, endured hardships, or faced death, the transition from one year to another represents a significant turning point in our lives. These moments deserve our utmost attention and should inspire us to pray, reflect, and plan for the days ahead, guided by the grace of God. Reflecting upon the year 2023, let us consider its impact on our lives. What were our individual plans, desires and experiences throughout this year? What prayers did we fervently offer? What engagements and strategies did we pursue to bring our dreams to fruition?

It is equally essential to assess the successes and failures we have had in our personal lives, families, and in organisations that we are part of. What lessons did we glean from these triumphs and setbacks? By earnestly addressing these inquiries and delving into related aspects of our journeys, we can gracefully bid farewell to 2023 and usher in 2024 with clarity and purpose, ensuring a smooth transition to the New Year. Without thoughtful reflection and answers to these questions, our shift from the passing year to the coming year may be turbulent, challenging, and ultimately unfulfilling.

While we acknowledge that human planning alone cannot guarantee success, it's essential to remember our God–given wisdom in Proverbs 19:21, which reminds us that God's purposes ultimately prevail. Therefore, we should commit our plans to the Lord, as stated in Proverbs 16:3. This commitment is further reinforced by the reassuring words of John 14:13–14, which emphasise the power of prayer and the promise that God hears and answers our supplications. Simultaneously, it is crucial to emphasise the importance of strategic planning in our preparations. It is highly recommended to engage in a thoughtful and prayerful process that involves reviewing the past year, anticipating the upcoming one, evaluating our accomplishments, learning from our failures, identifying our strengths and weaknesses, recognising opportunities and challenges, and finally, establishing new goals and crafting action plans.

To cement all the above, it is needful to recognise that faith is a catalyst for producing tangible results, as conveyed in Mark 11:24. Keeping in mind that faith is nurtured through hearing God's word, as emphasised in Romans 10:17, we should develop a habit of reading the Bible and immersing ourselves in the teachings of the gospel on a daily basis. This consistent practice not only strengthens our faith but also informs and shapes our actions and engagements. It paves the way for holistic transformation, ultimately leading to improvements in various aspects of our lives. While this reflection is not comprehensive, it provides a valuable starting point, awakens us to avoid becoming overly fixated on the transient nature of time, our triumphs, or our setbacks.

Conclusion

It serves as a gentle but compelling reminder to prepare ourselves for the arrival of the New Year 2024, fortified by the empowering grace of our benevolent and loving God.
